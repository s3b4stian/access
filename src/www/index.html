Frequently asked questions

What is the purpose of a visitor registration site?

The visitor registration site serves to streamline and manage the entry of individuals into Governement and Military facilities. It enhances security, facilitates efficient check-ins, and provides valuable insights.

How does the visitor registration process work?

Visitors can pre-register using the platform providing necessary details, such as identification information and the purpose of their visit. The system then generates a pass, simplifying the check-in process. The visitors can check the status of the pass using the Visitor Status section of this portal.

Do I have to register every time I have to participate in an event or access the facilities?

If this is the first time you need to access the facilities, it is mandatory to register via the Visitor using the section Visitor In of this portal. If you have previously registered you can directly request an appointment using the section Have Appointment of this portal.

What should I do if my access request is denied?

Access request may be denied for various reasons, including security concerns, failure to meet entry criteria, incomplete or inaccurate registration, non-compliance with safety protocols, or specific restrictions imposed by the facility. If your access is denied, the portal provide information about the reason for denial and any necessary steps to address the issue, check the Visitor Status section of this portal.
